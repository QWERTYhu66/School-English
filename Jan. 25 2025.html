Aaron's Reading Reflection Log 2025

Jan. 16, 2025 - The 57 Bus

What is happening in the story so far?

The story so far explores Sasha's life, focusing on their journey of self-discovery and identity. It highlights their unique interests, friendships, and the support system that aids them in navigating their agender experience. Through chapters such as “Tumbling,” “Pronouns,” and “1001 Blank White Cards,” we witness Sasha's creative spirit, their connection with language, and the way their friends respect and embrace their identity.

What are your first impressions of the characters so far?

Sasha appears to be intelligent, creative, and highly introspective, with a strong passion for expressing themselves through language and games. Their tight-knit group of friends is very supportive, emphasizing mutual respect and acceptance, particularly concerning Sasha's gender identity. This reflects a warm and inclusive dynamic between Sasha and their peers.

What themes/issues have been explored/introduced so far?

The key themes in this book include gender identity, friendship and inclusivity, and creativity and self-expression among others.

What are your initial opinions/impressions of the book so far?

The book is both poignant and engaging, providing a thoughtful look at identity, friendship, and acceptance. Sasha's creative spirit, along with the unique narrative format, offers a fresh and relatable view on the challenges and successes of navigating gender in a supportive setting. It's insightful and encourages empathy, making it both educational and profoundly moving.
