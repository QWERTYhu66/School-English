Aaron's Reading Reflection Log 2025

Feb. 12, 2025 - The 57 Bus

Examining the setting allowed me to better understand the story, such as the contrast between the more affluent parts of Oakland and the poorer, more destitute part of Oakland. Our main character, Sasha, lives in an affluent neighborhood and goes to a small private school. However, our other main character, Richard, lives in a poor neighborhood that is influenced by local gangs. Richard goes to a big public school, where many students fall into trouble.
